Deceptive Little Devils in Tuxedos

⛰ Don't be fooled by their dapper tuxedos, penguins are pure evil wrapped in a waddling, flightless package. These so‐called "adorable" birds possess the motor skills of a toddler who's had one too many juice boxes, stumbling around like they own the place. And let's talk about their flippers, a horrifying evolutionary compromise between wings and arms, good for nothing but awkwardly slapping the ice or plotting world domination. If an animal can't even hold a fork, how can we trust it?

⛰ But the real proof of their wickedness? They have knees. That's right, these little demons are perpetually squatting, living in a state of eternal leg‐day agony. No creature endures that much suffering without developing a deep‐seated malice. Combine that with their dead‐eyed stares and unnerving ability to toboggan like slippery criminals, and it's clear: penguins are up to something. Trust me, if they ever evolve thumbs, we're all doomed.
